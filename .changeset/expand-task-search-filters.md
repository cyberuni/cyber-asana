Expand `task search` filters to cover the full Asana search API: `.not` and `.all` variants for assignee, project, section, and tag; portfolio, follower, created-by, assigned-by, liked-by, and commented-on-by filters; and date-range filters for due, start, created, completed, and modified dates. All new filters are exposed in both the CLI and the MCP tool.
